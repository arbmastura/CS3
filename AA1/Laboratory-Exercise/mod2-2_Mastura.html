Pokemon

Pokemon is an internationally known Japanese fantasy cartoon that revolves around fantastical creatures known as 'Pokemons.' The show revolves around the capture, taming, and domestication of these creatures. Since its launch, it has become a staple in pop culture, with its baseball cards, spinoffs, graphic novels, and various other merchandise.
